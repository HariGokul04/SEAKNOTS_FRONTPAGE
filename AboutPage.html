About Us

Olympia Tech Park, Guindy

Overview

With globalization at its peak, the tank container operations industry plays a pivotal role in the global transportation of liquid goods, offering a safe and efficient method for shipping various chemicals, food products, and other liquids.

However, the industry is filled with complexities like diverse container types, stringent regulations, and the need for streamlined logistics. That’s where SeaKnots fills the gaps. With our custom tank container solutions, we help container businesses overcome inefficiencies, ensuring precise container management, compliance with regulations, and enhanced overall operational efficiency.

Our Capabilities

Tank Container Depots

We design ISO tank container management solution here web based depot management systems to help you handle key operations of container depots. This includes gate moves, container survey and repair management. Software solution will help to make depot operations more efficient and covers various aspects like washing, pre-trip inspection, tracking, billing, enhancing reporting etc.

NVOCC Software

SeaKnots also designs software to fulfill the needs of Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers (NVOCC) with global operations. It’s tested for real time challenges in NVOCC operations and includes features like tariff management, equipment control, vendor contracts etc.

Container IT Solutions

We also design full-fledged container lease management solutions to handle everything from operations, sales, and procurement of containers. Our software’s is equipped to handle leasing of hundreds of containers be it small, medium, or large.
